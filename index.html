About Meridian

Expertise grounded in precision & compassion

Meridian Orthopaedics was founded by a team of fellowship-trained surgeons who believed private care should combine clinical excellence with genuine human connection. Today, we remain one of the UK's most trusted specialist clinics for upper limb conditions.

From initial consultation through surgery and rehabilitation, every step is guided by evidence-based protocols and delivered with meticulous attention. Our surgeons hold appointments at leading university hospitals and remain at the forefront of research and technique.

Our Services

Comprehensive specialist treatment

We focus exclusively on shoulder, hand, and upper limb conditions — offering depth of expertise that generalist clinics simply cannot match.

Shoulder Surgery

Rotator cuff repair, shoulder stabilisation, impingement, and arthroscopic procedures. Restoring full range of motion with minimal downtime.

Hand & Wrist

Carpal tunnel release, tendon repair, fracture fixation, and microsurgery. Delicate work demanding the highest level of surgical skill.

Sports Injuries

Return-to-play programmes for elite and recreational athletes. ACL-equivalent protocols applied to upper limb sport injuries.
